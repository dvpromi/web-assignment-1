Political instability grips the country with protests against the ousted leadership and calls for systemic reforms. The interim government is focused on restructuring key institutions to pave the way for democratic elections​.

Protests Rock Political Landscape

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh is witnessing widespread protests following the ousting of former Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina. Student groups and civil society are demanding democratic reforms, justice for past violence, and fair elections. The interim government is facing challenges in stabilizing the country amidst growing unrest and calls for accountability​.

Bangladesh Eyes Institutional Reforms

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

The interim government of Bangladesh is prioritizing reforms in key institutions, including the Election Commission, judiciary, and police force. These efforts aim to depoliticize governance and ensure free and fair elections. Reports from reform commissions are anticipated soon, outlining steps to strengthen democracy and accountability
